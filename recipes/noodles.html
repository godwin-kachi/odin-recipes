Noodles

Description

Chinese Noodles is made from a corn flour milled with moderate heat, it is best taken with low heat cooked onion and chicken

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Shrimps
  • Chicken
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil water for 2 minutes
  2. Add your noodles to the boiling water
  3. Cook for 4 mins
  4. Add Olive Oil
  5. Add Shrimp
  6. Add salt to taste, leave to boil for 3mins
  7. Garnish with chicken and serve hot
